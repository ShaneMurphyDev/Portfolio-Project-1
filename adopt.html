Ginge

This handsome little dude is roughly 6 years old. If I could use only one word to describe this fellow it would be Gentleman. Ginge loves to play, explore, and be active, but he also adores cuddling and spending quality time with his human

Cinnamon AKA Cinny

Cinny is a beautiful fluffy orange and white long haired cat. She's very playful and loves to run around and hunt inanimate objects. Plenty of laughs will be had with this furball around

Pumpkin

Pumpkin got her name from being found hiding inside her namesake during last halloween. She can be shy at first but underneath her timid exterior in an extremely loving and gentle soul. Pumkin would be ideal for those looking to make their house a home.

Asmodeus

It's a common misconception that black cats are bad luck, and Asmo is living proof of that. Sure to bring joy to his humans life this fellow likes to skulk and act like he's the boss (he is) asmo is an older gentleman so you can expect him to be relatively quiet and to sleep alot.

Rumple
