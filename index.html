Tralee, Co. Kerry

Our mission

We here at Kingdom Cats have made it our mission to rescue, nurture, and rehome stray cats and kittens here in Kerry. We're a strictly no-kill operation as we belief every cat has the perfect human for them out there, we just have to find them. Our operation is possible through the amazing work of our volunteers and public donations.

Why adopt?

From present day to all the way back to the ancient Egyptians, humans have always seen the many benifits of having a cat. Studies have shown that a feline companion improves happiness and quality of life. Here are some of the many wonderful parts of cat ownership:

  • Stress Reduction - There are many health benifits to owning a cat, most notably a measurable reduction in stress and cortisol (blood pressure) levels.
